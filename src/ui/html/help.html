Security & Privacy

ClashFox respects your privacy and avoids collecting personally identifiable information whenever possible. Settings, status data, and local caches are primarily stored on your device. Network access is only used when needed for update checks, logs, and controller-related information.

  • Local configurations and caches can be cleared at any time.
  • Regular usage does not proactively upload personal data.
  • Update checks only request version and release metadata.
